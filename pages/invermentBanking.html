Mergers and Acquisitions

Barclays’ Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) group delivers strategic advisory services to clients. Our capabilities cover the entire spectrum of strategic alternatives available to clients, including acquisitions, restructurings and special committee assignments.

Our M&A team works in partnership with coverage bankers in providing solutions, using a highly analytical approach, providing unique insights, expert tactical advice and quality execution to clients' most challenging issues.

Global Finance

Barclays has consistently been a leader in the fixed income markets, ranking among the top underwriters of fixed income securities in India. We take a comprehensive approach, working closely with clients to develop and implement tailored solutions to specific issues and problems. Our capabilities span the spectrum of fixed income products from bonds, loans to commercial paper and structured financing solutions.

Our Debt Capital Markets team works closely with coverage bankers to originate, structure and market fixed income securities to investors. Working closely with our risk management partners, we provide comprehensive financing solutions for non-investment-grade clients across all industry segments through the use of leveraged loans, high yield bonds, bridge financing and mezzanine debt products.

Our Loan Capital Markets team provides a focus of excellence for the origination, structuring, execution and distribution of all types of loans. Product capabilities include coverage of a variety of specialist industry sectors, and our expertise spans corporates, financial sponsors, and financial institutions.

Risk Solutions Group

Barclays Risk Solutions Group is one of the leading providers of risk management solutions to both local and multi-national corporates in India, consistently ranking amongst the top 3 in all market surveys. We advise corporates on managing their Foreign Exchange, Interest Rates and Commodity price risks by providing bespoke hedging solutions followed by best in class execution.
